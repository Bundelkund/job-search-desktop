# Acme GmbH — Solutions Architect (AI Adoption)

> **Important**: this is a **fictional** posting used as an example for the apply-skill workflow. Acme GmbH is not a real company.

**Location**: Berlin (hybrid, 2 days/week onsite)
**Seniority**: Senior
**Type**: Permanent, full-time
**Salary band**: EUR 80,000–110,000 + equity

## About us

Acme GmbH is a fictional Berlin-based scale-up building enterprise AI tooling. We're a 60-person engineering organization with customers across DACH banking, insurance, and pharma.

## The role

We're hiring a Solutions Architect to own the AI adoption layer between our product engineering team and our largest enterprise customers. You'll spend half your time in code (Python, occasionally TypeScript) and half in workshops with customer technical leadership.

## What you'll do

- Run discovery workshops with customer architects to understand existing data + AI infrastructure
- Build proof-of-value implementations (RAG, multi-agent workflows, custom evals)
- Translate findings back into product feature requests; close the loop with engineering
- Mentor 2–3 junior solutions engineers
- Own customer adoption metrics (time-to-first-value, expansion revenue, NPS)

## What we expect

- 5+ years building production AI / ML systems
- Strong Python; TypeScript or Go is a plus
- Hands-on experience with at least one of: RAG, multi-agent frameworks, fine-tuning
- Comfortable in front of a customer technical leadership team
- Fluent German + English

## Nice to have

- Experience with CrewAI, LangGraph, or similar agent frameworks
- Workshop facilitation background
- Banking / insurance / pharma domain knowledge

Send CV + cover letter to careers@acme.example.
